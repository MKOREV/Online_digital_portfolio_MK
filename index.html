Welcome to my portfolio!

I am Minqiu

This digital portfolio is intended for prospective GIS employers, such as ESRI or urban consulting organizations, seeking someone with mapping and spatial data analysis skills (me!). In this portfolio I showcase my abilities to analyze spatial data and create maps for various applications in the urban context. I want my portfolio to be professional and transparent to make it simple for employers to understand how I may benefit their team. It is not just about presenting my prior work, it is also about demonstrating my potential to have an influence in the GIS industry.

Why I want a digital portfolio:

  • Why limit my audience when a digital portfolio can connect me to the world?
  • Why settle for static when a digital portfolio lets my work come alive?
  • Why let my creativity stay hidden when I can showcase it 24/7 online?
  • Why rely on words alone when a digital portfolio lets my visuals do the talking?
  • Why keep reinventing the wheel when a digital portfolio is easy to update?
  • Why create boundaries when a digital portfolio removes them all?
  • Why let paper dictate my creativity when pixels offer endless freedom?
  • Why blend into the crowd when a digital portfolio can make me stand out?
