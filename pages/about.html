I am a first-year Master student in the MADE (Metropolitan Analysis, Design, and Engineering) program. My background is in Earth Sciences, but these days I am focused on tackling urban challenges. I am interested in things like transport networks, smart cities, and using data to design better urban spaces. I love working with spatial data and creating maps that tell stories about places and how we connect with them. Outside of my studies, I enjoy wondering in (foreign) cities and nature as well as photographing and drawing. I have always been fascinated by how visual design can communicate complex ideas, and I am eager to learn more about using these skills to make urban challenges and solutions clear and engaging for different audiences.
