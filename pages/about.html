About Me

Hi, I am Minqiu Korevaar, a first-year master student in the Metropolitan Analysis, Design, and Engineering (MADE) program. My studies focus on understanding how cities work and finding ways to improve them using data and creative thinking. I am particularly interested in exploring the systems that shape how cities function, with a focus on transport networks and smart city technologies. Smart cities, for me, are not just about technology but about finding ways to use data and innovation to create urban environments that work better for everyone. My goal is to contribute to projects that have a positive and lasting impact. During my studies, I have worked on projects that combine data analysis with creative problem-solving. For example, I have mapped political shifts to understand social changes and analyzed the accessibility of urban areas to identify barriers. These projects have helped me see how data can offer new perspectives and provide a basis for practical solutions to urban challenges. By combining data analysis and visualization, and a focus on people’s needs, I hope to help create cities that are not just smarter but also better places to live. I look forward to working with people and organizations who are likeminded. If that sounds like you, let’s connect!
