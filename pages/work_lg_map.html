Loses and gains map

Presidential elections USA 2016-2020

This project visualizes the changes in political support across the United States during the presidential elections of 2016 and 2020. Using a geospatial analysis approach, the map highlights the gains and losses for both the Republican and Democratic parties on a state-by-state basis. The visualization provides an insightful comparison of voting patterns and shifts in political dynamics over the two election cycles.
