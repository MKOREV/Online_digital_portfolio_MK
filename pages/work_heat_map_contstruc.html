Building construction year heatmap

Temporal analysis

This project visualizes a heatmap of building construction years in Amsterdam. By analyzing spatial and temporal data, the map highlights patterns in urban development over time. The heatmap provides insights into the historical growth of the city and helps identify different construction period areas.
